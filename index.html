Bookshelf

Towards the end of 2019 I got into the habit of audiobooking during my morning routine.
The purpose of this page is to keep a list of these books and maybe eventually start writing out some thoughts for each.
My interests so far have mostly been around biographies and autobiographies, as well as a little bit of philosophy and books about startups or businesses.
The titles are listed in descending order of recency.
The rationale behind going for audiobooks is that if I actually have time to sit down and read recreationally,
I have that ever-present obligation to be reading acadmic papers or studying textboooks.
So much to do, such little hours in a day...
