Bookshelf

Towards the end of 2019 I got into the habit of audiobooking during my morning routine. The purpose of this page is to keep a list of these books and maybe eventually start writing out some thoughts for each. My interests so far have mostly been around biographies and autobiographies as well as a little bit of philosophy. In addition to audiobooks, I try to make my way through textbooks as part of my grad school research. To a certain extent, I find that reading multiple diverse textbooks is a great way to optimize for breadth, as opposed to reading 20 different papers about a specific sub-area of machine learning that are all just linear combinations of each other.
